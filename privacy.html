Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Who We Are

KetMen40 (ketomen40.com) is a keto diet and wellness resource for men over 40. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Sign up for our free email subscription (name and email address)
  • Contact us via email

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited through analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver the free Ketomen40 Recipe Cookbook
  • Send you email updates about keto tips, recipes, and product recommendations
  • Improve our website and content

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Email Communications

When you sign up for our email list, you will receive emails from KetMen40. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email. We use AWeber as our email service provider. Their privacy policy is available at aweber.com.

Affiliate Links

KetMen40 contains affiliate links to third-party products. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps support our site and allows us to continue providing free content. We only recommend products we believe in.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies and tracking technologies including:

  • Google Analytics — to understand how visitors use our site
  • Meta Pixel — to measure advertising effectiveness
  • Affiliate tracking cookies — to properly credit referrals

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • AWeber (email marketing)
  • Google Analytics (website analytics)
  • Meta/Facebook (advertising pixel)
  • ClickBank (affiliate marketplace)
  • Stripe (payment processing)

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

KetMen40 is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
